Privacy Policy for Rulet Bot

This Privacy Policy governs the collection, use, and sharing of personal information by Rulet Bot, a Discord bot developed by Birran237. By using Rulet Bot, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect:
-Server ID and it's configuration
-Users ID that have used the bot, the time they will be inactive and if they have any current streak (temporarily)

How We Use Your Information
Users and servers ID are used to apply correctly the configuration of the bot and the correspondent timeout of the user.
The server ID is deleted after kicking the bot of the server, and the user info is deleted after 1 day of not using the bot
